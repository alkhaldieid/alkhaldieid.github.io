About Me

I am Eid Alkhaldi, a Certified Professional Data Scientist with a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Autonomous Systems. I have over 8 years of experience in research and industry, with a focus on image classification, AI-powered solutions, and workflow automation.

I am currently a Deputy Site Manager at Alhabib Trading Company and have co-founded an autonomous driving startup. My work spans various AI applications in medical image processing, autonomous vehicles, and optimization algorithms.
